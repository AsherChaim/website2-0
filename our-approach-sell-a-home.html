The Asher Chaim Approach

With Asher Chaim you get the benefit of working one-on-one with an experienced knowledgeable agent. Our dedicated team will help list, market and close on your home for sale, while saving you time and money from commissions. You will find your real estate experience to be seamless and cost effective compared to other realtors in the market. Asher Chaim agents will take you step by step through the process from initial listing for sale until day of close. Here is our approach.


1. Determine the Selling Price

One of the key roles of an Asher Chaim agent is to help determine the value of your home to ensure that the listing and sale of the property goes as smoothly as possible. Your trusted agent will inspect the home and advise on what price he or she determines is a fair price according to market conditions. This is done by conducting a Comparative Market Analysis or better known as a CMA. Once the price is determined and agreed upon. Your home is one step closer to being listed for sale.



2. Prepare for Showings

Once the price is determined and listing is executed in writing you are now ready to show. Your trusted Asher Chaim agent will handle all the heavy lifting from here by marketing and showing your home during designated times that work for you.



3. List and Review Offers

After the listing and showing of your home through many of our marketing channels, it's not before long your home will receive offers to buy. It is our agents priority to disclose any and all offers to you so you can make the best decision possible and close on the best deal available.



4. Home Inspections and Appraisal Period

It is common that once you receive and accept an offer the buyer will ask for an inspection and an appraisal of your home, with the industry standard being about a ten day period. During this time you must grant access to the property and comply with any and all agreed upon (in writing) requests to conduct these inspections.



5. Closing the Deal

Once all the inspections have been completed, all negotiations and contingencies of the contract are met and have been agreed upon by both parties, you are now ready to proceed to closing. Your trusted Asher Chaim agent will handle the rest, working with all parties involved including the title agent to ensure a smooth closing. Congratulations you home is sold!
