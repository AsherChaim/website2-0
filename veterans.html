Veteran Leadership

The American Veterans Group puts a special focus on America's Veterans and their families. Our Team consists of military veterans and their dependents. We understand the difficulties of military life...PCS, TDY, Leave en route... and we understand the lingo ...LES, RNLTD, DD214, 1700hrs. Veterans helping Veterans is a perfect match. And what better way to thank a Veteran than to hire one!

-Retired Master Sergeant Dave Howe, Designated Broker for Asher Chaim Real Estate

Our Veteran Team

David Howe is the Designated Broker of Asher Chaim Real Estate. He has been an AZ resident since 1995 and has been working in the Real Estate Industry since 2010. David spent 21 years in the USAF, and retired as a MSgt/E-7 in 2009. Experience with all facets of the Real Estate industry, he has worked with buyers, sellers, investors, and property managers. David also holds the Short Sale and Foreclosure Resource (SFR) certification. Being an avid golfer and outdoorsman, David enjoys showing his clients all that Arizona has to offer.
