Our Story

Asher Chaim (pronounced kai-m) was founded in 2017 by three real estate and technology driven professionals with over 40 years of real estate experience. Our core business was real estate investing with deal identification via our proprietary algorithms. We found the marketplace to be very fragmented with brokerages touting tech and even more importantly we found high quality representation by agents very hard to come by for our investment deals. After discussion with our great industry partners and long-time clients we felt an obligation as stewards of the real estate industry to launch a full-service real estate brokerage.

The partners at Asher Chaim (known as the AC Partners) are inspired and driven by the vision of building a superior client-centric real estate brand, that leverages technology and utilizes the latest marketing tools and resources to deliver an unmatched client experience in real-estate. This starts with the professional team we build. Asher Chaim believes by providing world-class training focused on professional growth, leveraging our suite of technology and paring that with the latest market data, every agent will find success and deliver high quality representation throughout their career with Asher Chaim. In turn this unlocks immense value for our clients, our people and our community.

Whether you are a prospective client, partner or a professional looking to build your career in real estate we welcome you to the Asher Chaim family where we L.I.V.E. by the commitment to unmatched service, goal acceleration and inspiration through Leadership, Integrity, Value Creation and Excellence.

Our Mission and Vision

Mission: Deliver high-quality representation, leveraging technology as an enabler to our clients and agents. Cultivate lasting relationships through unmatched real estate services creating a client centric real estate brand that unlocks value for our clients, our people and our community for decades to come.

Vision: Build a superior client-centric real estate brand leveraging technology and the best people to deliver unmatched client service
becoming the leading brokerage within the markets we operate.
