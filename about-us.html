Our Values

Founded in 2017 by three real estate and technology driven professionals, Asher Chaim was inspired and driven by the vision of building a superior client-centric real estate brand leveraging technology and utilizing the latest tools and resources to deliver superior client service becoming the go-to investment and brokerage company within the market. Asher Chiam believes that by accessing the latest market data and providing the right tools, services and education every agent will find success throughout their career. We have designed a unique brokerage model and fee structure built to benefit all agents alike to encourage growth and opportunity, this provides value to each individual agent, drives success and long term growth. As a result over the years Asher Chaim has formed partnerships with many industry leaders to help agents excel in a competitive market creating long lasting and valuable relationships with each client along the way. These strategies and relationships keep Asher Chaim at the forefront of the industry and secures its place in the market for years to come.
